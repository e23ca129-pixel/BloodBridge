About BloodSync

Connecting blood donors with those in need. Every drop counts, every life matters.

Our Mission

To create a seamless connection between blood donors and recipients, ensuring that no life is lost due to blood shortage. We strive to build a community of lifesavers who are ready to help those in need.

  • Save lives through timely blood donation
  • Create a reliable donor network
  • Ensure blood availability for emergencies
  • Promote voluntary blood donation

Our Vision

A world where every patient in need of blood receives it on time. We envision a future where blood shortage is eliminated through technology-enabled donor-recipient matching.

Donor Eligibility Criteria

You Can Donate If:
  • Age: 18-65 years
  • Weight: 50+ kg
  • Good health condition
  • Hemoglobin: 12.5+ g/dL
  • 56 days since last donation
You Cannot Donate If:
  • Recent surgery/illness
  • Pregnant or nursing
  • High-risk activities
  • Certain medications
  • Travel to endemic areas
